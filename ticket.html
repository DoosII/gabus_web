Buying of Tickets and Guides

Guide

How to book a ticket?

1.) Fill up all the information needed.(Bus Liner, Date, Time, Origin, Destination)

2.) After filling up the information, a certain amount will auto generate depending on the distance of your origin to your destination.

3.) After, click next to proceed to payment options.

4.) Once you are already in the payment options page, fill up the gcash name and account number of the bus liner you are going to ride. You can also scan the QR code to easily pay for the fare.

5.) After paying, don't forget to screenshot the gcash receipt because you will be needing this when you are already in the bus.

6.) After you are already done with step 5, click confirm, then a receipt from GaBUS will appear.

7.) Also, don't forget to screenshot the receipt you receive from GaBUS.

8.) You can now proceed inside the bus.

What to do when going inside the bus?

1.) When you are already done with the booking and the payment, you can now proceed to the bus liner in your ticket.

2.) Once you are in the bus, wait for the conductor to ask for payment, then that is the time that you will present your receipt from gcash and from GaBUS.

3.) Relax and Enjoy the ride.

Important Reminders:

- Always present two receipts to the driver or conductor. (Gcash receipt and GaBUS receipt)

- GaBUS receipt always has the logo of GaBUS on top of the summary of details.

- Your origin must match with your destination. This means that if your origin is from the north, then your destination must be on the north. You can't book your origin from south and destination to north.
